    overview
X-META's groundbreaking VR program ’Mission Ice Loong (Dragon): Full-Sensation VR, is the world's first ice and snow-themed immersive VR experience. This VR program boasts a strategic partnership with the world’s famous Harbin Ice and Snow World in China. The approach transcended traditional filmmaking and VFX, leveraging Vitally AI Studio's cutting-edge and well-rounded gen AI capabilities for half of the footage. This includes AI image generation, image-to-video conversion, advanced image processing, AI-generated background music, narration, and sound effects. For the first time, AI-generated content is seamlessly integratedwith actual in-game VR content, showcasing the immersive blend of reality and virtuality that the VR program offers.
I was responsible for developing and refining Gen-AI outputs based on the director’s script, including generating AI visuals, animating transitions, and assembling AI-generated audio and voiceover. I worked closely with the creative team to translate narrative beats into cohesive scenes, iterating prompts and compositing assets to match tone, pacing, and emotional rhythm. I also led the editing process, integrating these AI-generated elements with gameplay footage to produce a polished final cut.

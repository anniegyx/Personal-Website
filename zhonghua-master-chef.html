      overview
Zhonghua Master Chef is a satirical, fast-paced strategy board game set in a fictionalized Chinese culinary universe where players compete to rise through the ranks of a cutthroat kitchen hierarchy. Merging culinary creativity with tongue-in-cheek cultural commentary, the game challenges players to master “legendary recipes,” sabotage rivals, and navigate surprise events—all while dealing with traditional judges, trending food fads, and sabotages from envious sous-chefs.

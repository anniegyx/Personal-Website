Overture
Documentary

A personal portrait film inspired by Ryuichi Sakamoto, meditating on his music’s ability to intertwine life, death, and renewal. Through soundscapes, rhythm, and visual layering, the film echoes Sakamoto’s sensibility while expressing your own dialogue with his legacy.

No Tomorrow
Short Film

A visual meditation on the Rubik’s Cube, using its shifting colors and endless permutations as a metaphor for choice, uncertainty, and the fleeting nature of time. The film blurs puzzle and poetry, exploring how even the smallest moves can change the shape of tomorrow.

20
Short Film

A reflective coming-of-age piece that captures the tension of turning twenty, weaving together memory, identity, and aspiration to portray the fragile space between youth and adulthood through fragmented vignettes and lyrical imagery.

Hold On & Let Go
Short Film + Creative Coding

A poetic exploration of grief and release, this project visualizes the emotional cycle of holding on to memories while learning to surrender. Through symbolic gestures, shifting perspectives, and atmospheric tone, the film captures the paradox of loss as both weight and liberation.

The Beauty Myth
Video Essay

A digital argument project about appearance anxiety and beauty shaming prevalent in current society. I want to find out how present-day social media platforms objectify women through makeup and beauty standards, in order to help the audience understand how the systematic nature of the male gaze’s influence, define, and dehumanize women over time.

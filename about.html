Hi, I'm Annie. I'm currently a junior studying Media Arts + Practice in the School of Cinematic Arts at the University of Southern California, minoring in Artificial Intelligence Application and Fashion . I'm interested in interaction design, spatial design, and fashion.

I like to call myself more of a storyteller. Growing up in multiple different culture backgrounds — born in Suzhou, China, attending middle school and high school in Philadelphia, and then finally coming to LA, I'm especially fond in creating works that focus on the intersection of different culture and identities. With apassion for visual storytelling, interactive design, and AI-powered creativity, I explore the space where design, technology, and culture meet, crafting experiences through motion graphics, digital media, and experimental tools. Welcome to my world of curious making.

I love photography, both digital and film. I also love everything about fashion and tennis. Outside of design, you can find me making coffee or cooking while listening to R&B.

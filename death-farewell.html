project
      overview
"Death & Farewell" tells a story of what happens after one's death through a nonlinear diary-entry styled website.
Death is not a permanent farewell. We might meet each other again in another dimension. Thus, a funeral becomes an emotional connection between the living and the dead. In “Farewell”, The viewers will first start their journey as a ghost, exploring in the dark. One day, the ghost suddenly spots on a funeral and starts its magic story from there. Through revealing the stories from the living loved ones, the ghost also get to respond to them. And it is last revealed that the ghost is actually you, and this is your funeral. In this project, I seek to explore the intricate relationship between live and death and also tell a story about a kid deprived of his/her dream under the increased revolutionized society.
